Quick Flow

1. Open WhatsApp Web and wait until the account is fully logged in.
2. Pick numbers, chats, contacts, groups, labels, or country audiences.
3. Write or paste the message and check the preview.
4. Set delays and batch pauses to match your campaign pace.
5. Start the run and watch progress both here and inside WhatsApp Web.
